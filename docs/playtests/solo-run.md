Start each participant on a fresh profile at the main menu. Give no control or scoring explanation unless they explicitly ask. Observe one first run and allow the session to continue until they choose to stop.

## What to record

For each participant, record:

- Time to first meaningful movement; target ≤20 seconds.
- Whether they state the run goal correctly without help.
- Control errors, camera corrections, missed gates, and misunderstood feedback.
- Whether they can explain why their score changed after the result appears.
- Whether they voluntarily select Play again; do not prompt them.
- Their first confused moment and their strongest positive reaction, in their own words.
